Seismicity at Rotokawa for the years 2012-2015 colored by date of occurrence. Blue events occurred earlier in the dataset and pink events occurred later. Four cross sections are plotted to show the depth distribution of seismicity and their surface projections are shown in map view. The dot-dashed diamond indicates the area of dense seismicity identified by \citealp{Sherburn_2015,Sewell_2015WGC} for the 2008-2012 catalog.
