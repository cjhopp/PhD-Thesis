Schematics of the operation of three types of geothermal power plants: A) Flash-steam, B) Binary and C) Combined cycle. At Rotokawa, the original 'RGEN' plant is type C, while the more recently-installed Nga Awa Purua plant is a 'triple-flash' variant of type A, meaning that there are three separate stages of liquid-steam separation, which increases plant efficiency \cite{DiPippo_2016}. The Ngatamariki plant is type B. These figures are adapted from the Ormat 2017 Annual Report \cite{Ormat_2018}
