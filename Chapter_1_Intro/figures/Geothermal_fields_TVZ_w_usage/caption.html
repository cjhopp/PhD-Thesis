The resistivity boundaries of the 23 geothermal fields in the Central Taupo Volcanic Zone (CTVZ) (after \citet{Bibby_1995}) colored by degree of development or geothermal operator. Rotokawa and Ngatamariki are labeled 'R' and 'N', respectively. The boundary of the young TVZ, as defined by \citet{Wilson_1995} ,  is shown as a dotted line and their eight identified rhyolitic calderas are outlined in white (Whakamaru Caldera labeled 'WH', Taupo Caldera labeled 'TP'). The Taupo-Reporoa Basin (TRB) is outlined in thick black after \citet{Downs_2014}.
