Cumulative frequency-magnitude distributions of the GNS Science catalog of templates (dotted lines) and the final matched-filter catalog (solid lines) for 2012-2015. Northern and southern Ngatamariki (Nga N, Nga S) are shown in black and gray, respectively. The magnitude of completeness is calculated using the maximum curvature method \citep{Wiemer_1997} . Calculated values of MC and b are noted in the top right.
