Overview of the Ngatamariki geothermal field. Injection wells are shown in blue, production wells in red with dots representing the wellhead and lines showing the surface projection of the well tracks at depth. Wells NM04, NM08, NM05 and NM07 are near vertical and, therefore, appear only as dots in the figure.  Active faults from the GNS Active Faults Database \citep{AFDB} are shown in black. The most likely boundary of the deep resource as published by \cite{Boseley_2010} based on magnetotelluric surveys, is shown in yellow. The northern and southern portions of the field, as referred to in this work, are divided at -38.55 degrees latitude and labeled here.
