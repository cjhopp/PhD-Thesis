Example detections of two events by template 2012sora469246 (signal-to-noise ratio of ~50 and ~3 respectively). The 1 s templates (in red) are overlain on 10 s of continuous data around the time of detection (black). The ML 0.62 template event occurred on 22 June 2012 and detected mostly events that occurred during the stimulation of well NM08 between 6 June and early July. The detection on the left represents a detection of a separate, yet highly-similar, template event (template 2012sora469256) which occurred approximately ten minutes after the template event shown here. The detection on the right is a newly-detected event.
